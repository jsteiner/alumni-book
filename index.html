Welcome to Metis!

Intro text

Justin Kenyon

Curious, musician, dreamer

What's your favourite movie? Shawshank Redemption

If you could have dinner with one person, dead or alive, who would it be and why?

I have been really deep into an Ancestry.com hole as of late and would love to meet my great grandfather from Ireland. I would ask him about life in America back then and definitely get some pictures of the family.

What's one skill that you could teach everyone else in class?

Installing an aftermarket stereo in your car. Or, something that can be done inside: a great card trick that ends in me slapping cards out of your hand and leaving the card you picked.

What is the one thing about you that people would be surprised to learn?

I am the lead singer in a BAND!band that has been playing in the Boston area over the last 10 years ... and I am huge fan of karaoke outings.
